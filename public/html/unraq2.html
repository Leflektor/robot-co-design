ROBOT CO-CREATION

Project Goal:

The goal of this project is to implement an internet site to assist in the co-design of medical robots, primarily aimed at helping older adults but also benefiting individuals with various illnesses who need such assistance from robots. The implemented site should facilitate the co-design process in such a way that even a person completely inexperienced with robots or technology can understand why certain features would be helpful for them. We aim to achieve this utility by providing a site with many well-explained pictures and a questionnaire that will help users design a robot perfectly suited to their needs.
